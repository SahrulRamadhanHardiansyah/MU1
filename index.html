Chelsea achieved their first success when they won the Premier League Division 1 title in 1955. This club won the English League Cup title for the first time in 1965 , the FA Cup title for the first time in 1970 , and the European club competition title, the European Cup Winners Cup for the first time in 1971 . Several titles from various competitions were also won in the 1990s, 2000s, 2010s and 2020s.

The last 25 years have been Chelsea's best period of success, winning five Premier League titles, including winning the Premier League and FA Cup double for the first time in 2010; seven FA Cup titles , four English League Cup titles , two UEFA Champions League titles , two UEFA Europa League titles , two UEFA Super Cup titles , and one FIFA Club World Cup title .

The club is one of five clubs to have won all three of UEFA's major club competitions, the first English club to do so. Chelsea is also the only club from London to have won the UEFA Champions League and FIFA Club World Cup.
